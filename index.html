Well Hello There!

Throughout my entire life, I questioned why and how things work with a strong desire to make them better.

Career wise, I used to work in tech support for AppleCare and later Squarespace. I loved helping customers, however I discovered that my job centered around asking an engineer to fix a bug or UX issue.

Because of my desire to provide a better customer experience and to take matters into my own hands, I began teaching myself how to program and got hired as a software engineer at Cascade Energy working on a proprietary Energy Management Information System called SENSEI.

This coding blog is meant as a personal reminder of my commitment to life long learning, improvement, and creating things. Creating is not about being better than someone else...it's about being better than you used to be.

Thanks for visiting my site! If you'd like to chat more, feel free to contact me via my personal website or social media links below.
