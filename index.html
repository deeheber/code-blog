Well Hello There!

Throughout my entire life, I've always questioned why and how things work with a strong desire to make them better. Career wise, I've been working in tech support. I love helping customers, but found that my job centered around asking an engineer to fix a bug or UX issue.

Wanting to have a deeper understanding of the engineers' point of view, I began doing some online coding tutorials and attended a coding bootcamp. I've been hooked on code ever since!

Having a strong belief that technology can enrich prople's lives, I've made it my persional mission to contribute everything that I can to help make the web a better place.

When I'm not dreaming up or building my next project, you can most likely find me enjoying a cup of coffee along with a book.

Thanks for visiting my site! If you'd like to reach out to me, feel free to contact me via my personal website or social media links above.
